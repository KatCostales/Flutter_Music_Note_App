Problem:

Music or audio software is expensive. It is difficult to find a convenient, cheap, and user-friendly tool that transcribes ideas when trying to write music. We strive to create an application that will effectively transcribe audio input into an editable music sheet. In this way, our software will allow users to freely and conveniently generate/compose music. Additionally, the listening and recording music relationship can be utilized to train users on how to read sheet music.

Objectives and Goals

Develop a software application with a user-friendly interface that will allow users who are interested in honing their musical talents/skills through an engaging app that challenges, tests, and teaches musical aspects to potential users/audiences (potential/target users are specified below).

Our team hopes to have this software application be compatible with Android and iOS users primarily in the United States. Additionally, we hope to have audio software compatible across multiple instruments. However, for the sake of our working time period, we will focus on developing our software to be compatible with the guitar, piano, and ukulele. Future implications or features include music sheet challenges, visually displaying chords/notes based on audio input analysis, audio analysis based on sound input, music sheet teacher, and more. Æ’

Target Audience:

Musicians (any level of expertise/experience) with limited financial budgets who have a desire to write/compose music. Other potential users include individuals who want to learn how to read sheet music without an instructor.

Device requirements:

(Android) smartphone and/or (iOS) iPhone users

System requirements:

Data storage, MyAccess, SQL

Specifications:

Receive audio input being played on an instrument and automatically transcribe to sheet music.

Have a user interface that allows users to edit/refine auto-transcribed music sheets

Include learning mode similar to flash cards where users will be shown a note on the music staff and be asked to play it on their particular instrument.

Compatible with Android and iPhone devices

Save scores created by users

Potentially create user profiles with data storage management.

Database storage needed. MyAccess or SQL.
